Challenge 3: Encryption - Cracking the Cipher

What is a Caesar Cipher?

Imagine writing secret messages that only your friends can decode! That's what encryption is all about.

The Caesar Cipher is one of the oldest tricks in cryptography. It's named after Julius Caesar, who used it to protect his military messages over 2,000 years ago!

How It Works:

Each letter in your message "shifts" a certain number of places in the alphabet.

How to Play

Your Mission

Decode secret messages by finding the right shift value! Can you find the right number?.

Cybersecurity Skill

Understanding encryption helps you protect important information like passwords and personal data!

💡
Tip: Look for common words that might appear in the message. Letters like 'E', 'T', and 'A' are the most common in English.
