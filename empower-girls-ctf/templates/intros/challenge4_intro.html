Challenge 4: Hidden Passwords

Why Passwords Matter

Trying to remember dozens of passwords is like juggling flaming marshmallows, fun for about a second, then chaos!

Many people (even adults!) use the same password everywhere or pick something too easy to guess.

Moose know the smartest way is a password manager, your very own digital vault that never forgets.

Here at Rapid7, we uncover millions of stolen passwords every day. That's why we need to learn to lock yours up safely!

Protect your digital secrets!

Password Security Tips:
Unique Passwords

Never use the same password on multiple websites

Complex Combinations

Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

Password Manager

Use a secure app to remember all your passwords

How to Play

Your Mission

You'll need to find Fred's password! He's lost it somewhere on his blog.

Password Detective
