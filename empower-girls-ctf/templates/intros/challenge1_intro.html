Challenge 1: Spot the Phish

Protect Yourself from Phishing

Every day, Rapid7's Moose protect businesses from phishing attacks — fake emails designed to steal secrets. By spotting the signs early, we keep the digital world safer!

What is Phishing?

Phishing is when someone sends a message pretending to be a trusted source, but they're really trying to trick you into:

  • Giving away personal information
  • Clicking dangerous links
  • Downloading harmful files

Learn to spot fake emails!

Warning Signs to Watch For:
Suspicious Senders

Email addresses that look almost right but have small mistakes

Spelling Errors

Legitimate companies rarely make spelling mistakes

Urgent Threats

Messages that say "Do this NOW!" to scare you

Strange Links

Hover over links to see where they really go

How to Play

Your Mission

You'll see a series of emails. Some are real, some are traps! Look carefully at each email for the warning signs.

Make Your Choice

Click PHISH if it's suspicious, or SAFE if it looks okay!

💡
Security Tip: Always think before you click! Even if an email looks like it's from someone you know, be careful with unexpected links or attachments.
